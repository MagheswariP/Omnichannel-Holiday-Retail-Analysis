# Omnichannel-Holiday-Retail-Analysis
The holiday season is the biggest moment for retailers—customer expectations soar, behavior shifts quickly, and every channel matters. With shoppers moving fluidly between online browsing, mobile purchases, store visits, pickups, and returns, analyzing performance becomes both challenging and full of opportunity.

In this Report, I uncovering the story behind a fictional retailer’s holiday results. 

My goal: reveal insights across channels and recommend strategies to boost revenue, streamline operations, and elevate the customer experience. 

#  Project Details  
In this project, I explored a complete festive season retail dataset. The company operates sales across Web, Stores, and Marketplaces and runs a series of Christmas and holiday marketing campaigns from Black Friday through New Year. 
The dataset captures everything that happens during this period: promotions, customer orders, deliveries, inventory levels, and returns. My goal is to uncover insights that help improve holiday campaign performance, customer experience, and operational efficiency.

What I explored from the dataset:
I explored how each channel contributes to the bigger picture, identified the patterns in customer behavior, measured operational efficiency, and understood the strengths and weaknesses of the retailer’s holiday execution.


The dataset contained more than 40,000 records across multiple tables.  I used PowerBI  to clean, transform, and model the data using Power Query and DAX.
